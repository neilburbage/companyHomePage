About Us

If your interested in game development and creating video games you've probably heard of Unreal Engine. The Unreal Engine is a game engine developed by Epic Games. Here at ueHob we love unreal Engine and see it as an awesome platform at the forefront of the latest design technology. Fortunately for us, Unreal Engine provides, free of charge, the finest of 21 century software tools that enable web developers, game developers, designers and many others to use their abilities via this design process to produce truly outstanding work. Our talented creative team are dedicated to continually expanding exsiting skills as well as learning new development skills. Our goal is to continually produce original, high quality cutting edge work in the fields of web development and electronic music.
